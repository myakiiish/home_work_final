Brewing Guide

Proper brewing is important because it brings out the full spectrum of flavors.

Measure Your Tea

Use 5 grams of Diān Hóng tea leaves for every 150 ml of water to achieve the perfect balance of flavor and aroma.

1/4

Heat the Water

Bring water to 90–95°C (194–203°F) to preserve the delicate flavors and avoid bitterness during brewing.

2/4

Steep to Perfection

Steep the leaves for 20–30 seconds for the first infusion, gradually increasing the time with each brew to explore the evolving flavors

3/4

Savor Multiple Infusions

Relish each infusion as the tea’s complex profile unfolds, revealing new layers of taste with every cup.
