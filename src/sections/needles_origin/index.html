Yunnan Province, located in the southwestern part of China, is renowned for its rich biodiversity and varied landscapes, ranging from mountainous terrains to lush forests. This region is the cradle of numerous tea varieties, with a tea cultivation history spanning over a millennium.

Diān Hóng Sōng Zhēn, also known as "Pine Needle" black tea, originates specifically from Fengqing County in the Lincang Prefecture of Yunnan. The area's high altitude, fertile soil, and favorable climate contribute to the unique characteristics of this tea.
